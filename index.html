Executive Commentary

TTM May 2025 – Apr 2026 — Key Observations:

  • 🔴 Sev 1 case volume increased +63.4% YoY (from 101,503 to 165,880) over the trailing 12 months, even as overall case inflow declined by 8.6%. This surge appears to be driven primarily by the open flexibility for anyone to create Sev 1 cases without a validation step, rather than a genuine rise in product-related critical issues.
  • 🔬 Investigation rates remained flat at ~4.0% — unchanged from the prior year — confirming that the growth in Sev 1 volume is not correlated with an increase in underlying product issues; a stable investigation rate alongside a +63% Sev 1 spike is a strong signal that the increase is behavioural, not technical.
  • 📊 Across 331,888 accounts, 34,131 (10.3%) raised at least one Sev-1 case in the last 12 months, generating 165,845 Sev-1 cases in total.
  • 🏆 AMER REG drives the highest absolute Sev-1 volume (16,467 cases), while SASIA shows the highest case intensity.
  • ✍️ Notably, 59.2% of Signature accounts (3,626 of 6,129) created Sev-1 cases — representing just 1.8% of the account base but contributing 15.6% of all Sev-1 cases.
  • 📈 A heavy-tail pattern is evident: accounts filing 6+ cases (5,582 accounts, 16.3% of creators) generate 69.2% of all Sev-1 volume — making them the primary lever for proactive reduction.
  • 🏢 Top Sev-1 creators include Amazon.com, Inc. (334 cases) and KOTAK MAHINDRA BANK LIMITED (238 cases).
